# Reserving Spaces

All room reservations must done through beInvolved [25live](https://one.iu.edu/task/iu/25livepro), unless booking the
community center.
- The community center booking form can be found under the [LSG
SharePoint](https://indiana.sharepoint.com/:u:/r/sites/O365-Luddy-Student-Government-2526/SitePages/Staff-and-Student-Leaders.aspx?csf=1&web=1&e=3s9BVy). If you do not have access to this page, be sure to contact [LSG](mailTo:lsg@iu.edu)

If you wish to reserve a space not included on this list, reach out to the [Luddy Student Engagement Office](contact.md) for information on how to reserve other spaces.
